CaveRunner is a simple 2D sidescrolling infinte runner made to get familiar with Unity's 2D tools.

Even though its a simple game it gets challenging and can be quite fun, i catched myself wasting time playing instead of testing and developing.

You can get the PC Version from the github repo, there are also the credits for the materials i'm currently using. I'm planing to make every graphic and sound myself later on.

« created with Unity »

Currently the music is autoplaying without a possibility to turn it off, since i know how annoying that is i will add a volume slider soon.

How to play:

Use space to jump, press space again to double jump

Your double jump recharges if you touch a plattform

Collect gold and cloverleafs to increase your points

Avoid Zombie-eyes because those will get you minus points
